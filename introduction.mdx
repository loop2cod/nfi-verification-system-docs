Integrating NFI Clear takes just a few minutes.

1.  **Get API Keys**: Sign up for an account and generate your API keys in the dashboard.

## Products

*   **Identity Verification**: Verify government-issued IDs and liveness.
*   **Document Verification**: Validate proof of address and corporate documents.
*   **Liveness Detection**: Prevent spoofing with 3D face maps and 5-pose active liveness.
*   **Business Verification (KYB)**: Automate corporate due diligence.
